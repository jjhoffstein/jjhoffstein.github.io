Individual & Group Lessons

Available dates and times for individual and small group lessons vary.

Lessons are generally held at the Greens Farms Academy Coyle Gymnasium.

Please email (jjhoffstein@gmail.com) or call (610-731-3122) to reserve a date and time.

For individual lessons, each player will be put through a series of drills to evaluate the strengths and weaknesses in their game and then receive a personalized workout to practice efficiently on their own. Individual training is also effective for improving shooting technique.

For group lessons, workouts will consist of intense 1 v 1 and 2 v 2 games focusing on learning to read the defense quickly, efficient footwork, and finishing through contact.
