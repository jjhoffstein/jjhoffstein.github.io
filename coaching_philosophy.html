My Coaching Philosphy

The purpose of my coaching philosophy is to (1) articulate my goals, and (2) let you know how I plan to achieve those goals.

After many iterations, I managed to boil down my coaching philosophy into one single sentence. That sentence is:

I aim to foster student-athlete success by building a culture of basketball excellence where the goals are meaningful work and meaningful relationships achieved through core values of truth, love, and attention.

The bullets below will help you better understand the different elements of my coaching philosophy. Although the bullets are not fully comprehensive, they will give you a better sense of what my coaching philosophy is all about.

Student-Athlete Success

  • We define success as improvement over time, i.e. “success is a journey”.
  • We know that success includes “the whole person”, not just the athlete.

Culture of Basketball Excellence

  • We care about being great and strive to discover our best selves.
  • We relish competition.
  • We hold each other accountable to high standards of behavior.

Meaningful Work

  • We are on a mission together, which energizes us.
  • We view learning through failure as a thrill to be cherished, not as a chore to be avoided.

Meaningful Relationships

  • We maintain mutual care and respect for each other.
  • We perform acts of loving kindness towards each other without expecting anything in return.
  • We stick by each other through thick and thin.

Truth

  • We put our honest thoughts on the table; we are transparent and real with each other.
  • We value individual integrity over all other character traits.
  • We believe that having individual integrity leads to mutual trust which provides an optimal environment for improvement over time.

Love

  • We strive to respond in a loving fashion no matter the situation.
  • We recognize that whereas trust must be earned, love is something that can be freely given.

Attention

  • We value the ability to pay attention above all other skills.
  • We focus on things that other teams normally take for granted, which gives us a tremendous competitive advantage.
